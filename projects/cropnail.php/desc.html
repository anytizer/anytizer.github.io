Generate better thumbnails of any dimensions, without losing the original aspect ratio.

As CLI Tool

Cropnail also has a command line interface to organize your photos. It features to group the photos by their dimensions, resize images and create thumbnails - all in command line.

Usage examples

cropnail list

cropnail group

cropnail 200x450 source.png target.png

cropnail 400x200 source.png target.png

It can find out wide or tall images by the image dimension (and not by its contents).
